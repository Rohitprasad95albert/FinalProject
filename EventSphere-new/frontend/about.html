About EventSphere

EventSphere is your university's central hub for discovering, organizing, and engaging with campus events. Built to be fast, modern, and student-friendly.

Fast & Intuitive

A clean, responsive interface powered by Tailwind CSS for an effortless experience on any device.

Community First

Connect students, clubs, and admins through events, notifications, and certificates.

Reliable & Secure

Robust authentication and admin tools keep the platform safe and organized.

What You Can Do

Students

  • Discover and filter events
  • Register and manage reminders
  • Give feedback and earn certificates

Clubs

  • Create events with rich details
  • Track attendees and verify presence
  • Upload certificate templates
